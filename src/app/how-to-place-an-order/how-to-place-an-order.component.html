How To Place An Order?

Kubona.ng is an online catalog showcasing our available products. Every product listed on the website is in stock at our warehouse. Here are the various methods for shopping through kubona.ng:

1. Shop at our warehouse:

  • Visit our physical shop located at Oniru, Lekki. You can find directions and our opening hours by clickingÂ here.

2. Shop by website

  • Browse through our online catalog.

  • Click on the image of the desired item to access the product details page.

  • Select your size and/or color and click "Buy now." This action will take you to the order page.

  • Enter your phone number and click "Order via WhatsApp."

  • You'll be directed to WhatsApp, where we'll process your order.

  • Expect delivery within 1-3 business days in Lagos, or 3-7 business days outside Lagos through our third-party courier partners.

3. Shop by WhatsApp

  • Browse our online catalog.

  • Take note of the product description or code of the item you desire, and take a screenshot if possible.

  • Send the code (and the screenshot if available), along with your size, name, and delivery address, to 07036110000 via WhatsApp.

  • We will process your order and send order details to you through WhatsApp.

  • Expect delivery within 1-3 business days in Lagos, or 3-7 business days outside Lagos via our third-party courier partners.

4. Shop by Phone call

  • Browse through our online catalog.

  • Note the product description or code of the item you wish to purchase.

  • Call us at 07036110000 between 9:00 am and 6:00 pm Nigerian time.

  • Provide the agent with the product code, your delivery address, and your preferred payment method.

  • Expect delivery within 1-3 business days in Lagos, or 3-7 business days outside Lagos via our third-party courier partners.
